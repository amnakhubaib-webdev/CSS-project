HARRY POTTER

Harry potter is a series of seven fantasy novels written by british author j.k.rowling.The novels chronicle the lives of a young wizard,Harry potter,and his friends HermioneGranger and Ron weasley,all of whom are students at hogwarts school of witchcraft and wizardry.


THE AUTHOR

Joanne Rowling born 31 July 1965, also known by her pen name J. K. Rowling, is a British author and philanthropist. She wrote Harry Potter, a seven-volume children's fantasy series published from 1997 to 2007. The series has sold over 500 million copies, been translated into at least 70 languages, and spawned a global media franchise including films and video games.
